Scientific Foundation

Built on real research,
positioned as art

Lumaphoria is "science-inspired art" — we don't make clinical claims. The underlying technology draws from peer-reviewed research on neural entrainment, fractal perception, and brainwave synchronization.

Neural Entrainment

The brain naturally synchronizes to external rhythmic stimuli — the Frequency Following Response. Specific flicker frequencies correspond to specific brainwave states: 10Hz alpha for calm, 40Hz gamma for focus, 4-8Hz theta for creative/meditative states.

Multi-Sensory Stacking

Overlapping frequencies across visual, auditory, and haptic channels creates whole-brain coherence that individual frequencies cannot achieve alone. This is the core innovation from 15 years of hardware R&D.

Fractal Fluency

Research shows fractal patterns reduce physiological stress by 60% and match the brain's natural visual processing preferences. Lumaphoria's visuals are built on fractal geometry tuned for therapeutic response.

Research Partnerships
